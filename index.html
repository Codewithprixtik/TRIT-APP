About the Author

PRATIK SHUKLA

Trader · Educator · Storyteller

Pratik Shukla is a passionate trader and educator who believes that financial literacy should be taught from a young age. Having navigated the complexities of the Indian stock market himself, he created That Rookie Indian Trader to make the learning journey fun, visual, and accessible for everyone — especially kids and young adults just starting out.

Through his website thatrookieindiantrader.com, Pratik shares insights, blog posts, and resources to help a new generation of Indian investors grow their wealth with confidence.
